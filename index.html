Merry Circles

This is a simple game. On the first page you can to chose the color of your circle and to input the circle's name. Then you can move your circle on the field using arrow keys and mouse. Unlimited number of users from different parts of the world can move the circles in the same field. I used html/css, js, jQuery, node.js, socket.io during development of this application. My friend helped me. Thanks to him. Don't mind the Spartan design of the first page. It is in production.
